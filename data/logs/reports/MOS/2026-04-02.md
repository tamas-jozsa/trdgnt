The geopolitical changes might enhance market demand short-term; however, escalating **input costs** threaten profit margins. Thus, without a solid operational base—exemplified by negative cash flow—Mosaic's current position leaves investors at considerable risk of capital loss, outweighing these potential benefits.

#### 5. **Conviction Score**

Investors should be cautious, particularly leading up to earnings reports that threaten to exacerbate these issues.
